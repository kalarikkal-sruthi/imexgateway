MEP & HVAC Materials

Comprehensive List Of Common MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) Materials:

Mechanical
Materials

  • Pipes (Copper, Steel, PVC, PEX)
  • Fittings (Elbows, Tees, Couplings)

Materials
